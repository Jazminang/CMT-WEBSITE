About Us

CMT, an acronym for Cut Make Trim, stands as a testament to quality and innovation since its establishment in 1988. With a rich legacy spanning over three decades, our clothing manufacturing factory has been at the forefront of the industry. We take pride in offering an extensive array of services, including knitting, dyeing, printing, washing, finishing, and embroidery, providing comprehensive solutions for all your apparel needs. Our unwavering commitment to excellence and sustainable practices has positioned us as a trusted partner in the fashion world. At CMT, we don't just create garments; we craft experiences, blending craftsmanship, and cutting-edge technology to deliver exceptional products that meet and exceed the highest industry standards.
