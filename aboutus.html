About Us

"CMT, an acronym for Cut Make Trim, stands as a testament to quality and innovation since its establishment in 1988. With a rich legacy spanning over three decades, our clothing manufacturing factory has been at the forefront of the industry."

Mission

Our mission is to craft experiences through exceptional apparel solutions, blending craftsmanship and innovation. We are committed to providing comprehensive manufacturing services that exceed industry standards, ensuring the success of our clients and the satisfaction of their customers.

Vision

Our vision at CMT is to remain an enduring symbol of quality and innovation in the clothing manufacturing industry. We strive to lead with cutting-edge technology and sustainable practices while continuously expanding our service offerings. We aspire to be the go-to partner for the fashion world, known for our unwavering commitment to excellence.

Purpose

Driven by our rich legacy dating back to 1988, our purpose at CMT is to empower the fashion industry with comprehensive apparel solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and sustainability. We are dedicated to helping our clients succeed by providing them with the tools to create remarkable, lasting experiences through the garments we manufacture.

Our Core Values

Quality Excellence. Sustainability. Craftsmanship. Partnership. Integrity. Customer Satisfaction. Continuous Improvement.
